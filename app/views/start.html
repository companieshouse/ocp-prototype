View company correspondence

Use this service to view certain Companies House correspondence online.

In this service you can view, download and print certain Companies House correspondence that is also sent to the registered office address in a paper format.

This includes:

  • reminder letters
  • penalty notices
  • acceptance letters
  • rejection letters
